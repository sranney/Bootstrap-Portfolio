Welcome to my Portfolio

My name is Spencer Ranney. I am from Austin Texas. I am a graduate of The University of Texas at Austin, where I received a Bachelors in Actuarial Math and a Masters in Finance. I have worked in finance and property insurance pricing. I have had the opportunity to work in political analytics, pricing analytics, healthcare analytics and investment performance analytics with self-taught coding skills. I strive for constant development, and look for new challenges. I love the combination of creativity and logic that coding requires. I believe that web development can be the best that coding has to provide in the mix of creative and logic. That is why I am pursuing a career in web development.


I am excited about mixing my data analysis skills and knowledge, and my coding skills with web development in order to come up with products that I can share online. If you have any projects that require either data analysis, web development or a combination of these skills, please connect with me.
